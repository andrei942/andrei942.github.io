Crypto Giveaway Scams

Crypto giveaway scams promise free Bitcoin or Ethereum if you send a small amount first. These are ALWAYS scams.

How They Work

  • Fake celebrity or company accounts
  • Promise to double your crypto
  • No real returns

How to Stay Safe

  • No legitimate giveaway asks for payment
  • Verify official websites
  • Never trust social media comments
